Vnc Server:
!!!Goruntulemek istedigimde desktop donuyor, olmuyor
raspberry side:
open the vnc server:
vncserver :1 -geometry 1920x1080 -depth 24
kill the vnc server when needed:
vncserver -kill :1

on local computer connect the vnc:
vncviewer <Raspberry_Pi_IP>:<display>
vncviewer 192.168.1.100:1
